Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about switching to solar

How much does a solar system cost?

The cost varies based on your energy consumption and the system size required. On average, a residential system pays for itself within 3-5 years through savings on electricity bills. Contact us for a free customized quote.

Are there government subsidies available?

Yes, the government offers attractive subsidies for residential solar installations. Our team will handle the entire documentation and subsidy claim process for you to make it hassle-free.

How long does the installation take?

A standard residential installation typically takes 2-3 days once the design is approved and materials are delivered. Commercial projects depend on the scale but we ensure minimal disruption to your operations.

What is the warranty period?

We use premium Tier-1 panels that come with a 25-year performance warranty. Inverters typically have a 5-10 year warranty, and we provide a 5-year workmanship warranty on our installation.

What maintenance is required?

Solar systems require very little maintenance. Occasional cleaning of the panels (to remove dust/bird droppings) is usually sufficient to maintain optimal efficiency. We also offer comprehensive Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC).
